RoboVerse 2026 Qualifier — Run Instructions (v9 mission)
==========================================================

WHAT THIS CODE DOES
-------------------
The drone takes off, then visits up to 12 "spots" in the world. At each
spot it does ONE slow 360° rotation (~15s) so the depth camera + YOLO can
see every direction, then sprints forward up to 12m to the next spot.
Spot selection biases toward unexplored headings using the depth clearance.
YOLO detections are projected into NED coordinates and deduplicated; the
output is written to /mnt/hgfs/Shared/barrels.json.

The mission bails at 280 s (under the 5-minute time-bonus cliff) and
returns to land. The competition rules allow multiple attempts within
the 10-minute window — best score counts. Plan to RE-RUN 2-3 times.


PRE-FLIGHT (one time on the judge's laptop)
-------------------------------------------
- Python deps: cv2, mavsdk, gz.transport13, gz.msgs10, torch, ultralytics, pymavlink.
- PX4 build: gz_x500_vision in ~/PX4-Autopilot.
- OakD-Lite SDF MUST be the lightweight 640x480 @ 10 Hz version
  (~/PX4-Autopilot/Tools/simulation/gz/models/OakD-Lite/model.sdf).
- barrel_yolo.pt MUST be present in ~/Desktop/codes/.
- ~/start_sim.sh, ~/stop_sim.sh, ~/set_ekf_origin.py executable.

WHAT JUDGES WILL SEE
--------------------
- A live "YOLO Detections" OpenCV window. Every time YOLO finds a barrel
  with conf >= 0.45, an annotated frame is shown with bounding boxes and
  the class name. This is the live evidence during the run.
- Annotated frames are ALSO saved to /mnt/hgfs/Shared/yolo_detections/
  (file names: det_<n>_<ms-timestamp>.jpg). These are persistent backup
  evidence for after the run.
- /mnt/hgfs/Shared/barrels.json with two lists:
    * barrels  (raw — all dedup'd detections)
    * validated  (the entries that meet the validation filter:
                 hits >= 2 OR confidence >= 0.75)
  Judges should look at `validated_total` and `validated_by_class` to
  count unique detections, NOT the raw counts (which include single
  low-confidence noise hits).


BETWEEN ATTEMPTS
----------------
Each attempt:
  Terminal 1:  ./stop_sim.sh
               ./start_sim.sh --no-qgc
  Terminal 2:  python3 ~/set_ekf_origin.py
  Terminal 3:  PROTOCOL_BUFFERS_PYTHON_IMPLEMENTATION=python python3 mission.py

The mission's BarrelTracker resets per-attempt — barrels.json gets
overwritten. To preserve a good attempt's output:
  cp /mnt/hgfs/Shared/barrels.json /mnt/hgfs/Shared/barrels_attempt_N.json
  cp -r /mnt/hgfs/Shared/yolo_detections /mnt/hgfs/Shared/yolo_detections_attempt_N

The competition counts the BEST attempt within 10 minutes (rule p.1):
> "the teams can make more than 1 attempt. The score will be based on the
>  best attempt."

KNOWN LIMITATIONS (be honest with judges if asked)
--------------------------------------------------
- x500_vision SITL's "VIO" is a noisy Gazebo-ground-truth feed that PX4's
  EKF can't reliably reject. Position estimates may drift ~5-15 m during
  a single 280-second flight.
- This means the NED coordinates in barrels.json are NOT absolute
  ground-truth positions; they are *consistent with each other for dedup*
  but offset from the world frame by the accumulated EKF drift.
- Practically, this caps how far the drone can effectively *navigate*
  toward distant barrels. Mid-mission re-runs (the multi-attempt rule)
  partially compensate.
- The drone is spawn-area-biased — its physical motion per flight is
  ~5-10 m even with 12m setpoint sprints. This is why we run multiple
  attempts.


TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------
- "is_armable=False" persists > 30 s:
    Re-run set_ekf_origin.py. If still failing, ./stop_sim.sh and start over.

- "Connection refused" to UDP 14540:
    PX4 isn't running. Verify with `screen -ls` (should show 'px4' session).

- "Socket closed" or "AioRpcError" mid-flight:
    PX4 crashed (rare, but happens after very long flights). stop_sim + start_sim
    + set_ekf + re-run mission.

- "depth all-inf / no-frame":
    Stop sim, restart sim, re-run set_ekf_origin, re-run mission. If still
    all-inf, OakD-Lite SDF may have been overwritten — restore from the
    /sdf-patches/ copy on the USB drive.

- "Address already in use" on port 14540:
    Stale mavsdk_server from a previous run. `pkill -9 mavsdk_server`.

- Drone flies into walls / spins / pose runs away:
    The EKF has diverged. Mission's _is_ekf_runaway check will catch this
    and abort, then re-land. If land also fails, manually run stop_sim and
    re-run.
